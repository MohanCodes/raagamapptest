Raaga Mohanam

Scale

The scale of Raaga Mohanam is:

S R2 G3 P D2 S

S D2 P G3 R2 S

Mood and History

Raaga Mohanam is known for its serene and devotional mood. It is a pentatonic scale and is often used in compositions that evoke a sense of peace and devotion.

Famous Compositions

  • "Nannu Palimpa" by Tyagaraja
  • "Kapali" by Papanasam Sivan
  • "Rara Rajeeva Lochana" by Tyagaraja
