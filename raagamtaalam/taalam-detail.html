Adi Taalam

Structure

Adi Taalam is an 8-beat cycle, structured as follows:

4 + 2 + 2

Practice Exercises

Practice keeping the Adi Taalam rhythm with these exercises:

  • Clap on the first beat, wave on the second beat.
  • Tap your fingers on the third and fourth beats.
  • Repeat the cycle, increasing speed gradually.
