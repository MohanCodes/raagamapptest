Glossary

Understand the terminology used in Carnatic music. Browse through the glossary to learn more.

  • Arohana: Ascending scale of a raaga.
  • Avarohana: Descending scale of a raaga.
  • Swaram: Musical note.
  • Taalam: Rhythmic cycle.
  • Raaga: Melodic framework for improvisation.
